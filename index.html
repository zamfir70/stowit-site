FAQ

How do I activate Pro?
On Mac App Store: tap Upgrade to Pro in Settings.
On other platforms: enter your license key in Settings.

How does Cloud Sync work?
Items sync automatically between devices sharing the same sync code. The relay is stateless — your data passes through but is never stored.

Is my data stored on a server?
No. All data is stored locally on your device. The cloud relay is stateless and encrypted in transit.

Which platforms are supported?
Windows, macOS, and Linux. iOS support is in development.
